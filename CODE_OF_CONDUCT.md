# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating and maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for everyone who participates in this project. We value contributions from developers of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

✅ **Do:**
- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Use inclusive language
- Be patient with others and offer help
- Welcome differing opinions and viewpoints
- Focus on constructive criticism
- Give credit where it's due
- Report issues privately if they're sensitive
- Assume good intentions

❌ **Don't:**
- Use offensive, discriminatory, or abusive language
- Harass or attack other community members
- Make discriminatory jokes or comments
- Spam or send unsolicited advertisements
- Share others' private information without consent
- Engage in trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Use this platform to promote hate or discrimination
- Ignore reports of misconduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Public spaces where project representatives act in an official capacity
- Unofficial spaces that represent the project or community

## Enforcement

### Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Report privately** to the project maintainers
   - Email: [contact info provided]
   - WhatsApp: +923339482234

2. **Provide details:**
   - What happened?
   - Who was involved?
   - When and where?
   - Any evidence or witnesses?

3. **All reports are confidential** and will be handled promptly

### Response

- All reports will be investigated fairly
- Inappropriate behavior will be addressed appropriately
- Serious violations may result in:
  - A warning
  - Temporary restriction from participating
  - Permanent removal from the project

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was unfair, you may appeal by:
1. Documenting your concerns in writing
2. Submitting to project maintainers
3. Receiving a response within 7 days
